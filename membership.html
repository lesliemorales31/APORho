Membership

Rush

Invites are distributed on the basis of points. Rush will be divided into 2 weeks: informal and formal rush.

During informal rush rushees will be required to attend at lease one social and service event.

During formal rush, rushees will are encouraged to attend as many socials as possible and must attend at least one service event.

Points can be recieved through visitations and through the number of service and social events attended during formal rush.

Pledging

Over the course of the semester pledges are required to complete 25 service hours as well as work on a pledge class prodject to raise funds for a nonprofit/NGO in the area.

During the course of the semester, they will also be meeting siblings and will recieve a Big.

Siblinghood

As a sibling you are required to complete 25 service hours. Additionally, siblings must attend 5 socials.

Events such as Philanthropy Night and Invite Night are mandatory.
